İTÜ ARIS LAB

Advanced Research on Intelligent Systems and Advanced Research on Images and Signals

ABOUT

Our laboratory specializes in various research areas of signal processing such as image and video processing, computer vision, noise, acoustic and bioacoustic signal processing, Deep Learning, Machine Learning, etc.


Prof. Dr. Tayfun AKGÜL heads and supervises members of ARIS LAB and AKAL LAB, allocated to Doctorate and Master's students and Bachelor's students respectively. Our Bachelor's students take part in new projects assisting their Doctorate and Master's fellow students while carrying out their internships simultaneously.
