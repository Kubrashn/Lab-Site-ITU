Seismology

High precision geophones are used in our study of Seismic events. The list of our previous projects include: Earthquake Data Analysis, Animal-Human-Vehicle Detection, Localization, etc.

Underwater

Underwater noise measurements are carried out using custom hydrophones. Time labeling of water transports, detection of sarine species, and extraction of underwater noise maps. The TÜBİTAK project "Analysis and Modeling of Underwater Sections in the Turkish Straits System" is being carried out in collaboration with Istanbul University, Institute of Marine Sciences and Management, Department Of Physical Oceanography And Marine Biology.
